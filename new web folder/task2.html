Rising Children of Ethiopia

Introduction

Rising Children of Ethiopia is a non governmental organization that help students and their family with providing edible products,cleaning materials like detergent every 2 months and school supplies every year.

People

When a heart is kind,even little things become big that can be shared with another.The organization is started by Wesene Gebremedhin. She is kind person living aboard and always dream to give and share what she got. Melaku, gash Ashenafi, etete Meseret, the volunteers are only few to be listed.
